1. Repost on your Facebook Page at Least Twice Per Day
 
Here's how:
 
 
 
 
 
Step 1
 
Log-in to Facebook.
 
 
 
Step 2
 
Find the Affiliate Referral post you want to repost.
 
 
 
Step 3
 
Press “Share” under the post.
 
 
 
 
 
Step 4
 
Use the drop-down box in the “Share This Status” dialog box to choose who you want to share the post with. You can repost to your own timeline, a friend's timeline, to a group, on a page you own or as a private message to another user.
 
 
 
Step 5
 
Compose a message of your own in the “Write something...” box. This isn't required, but offers the chance to add to the original message or explain why you're reposting. For Example "These guys are amazing!"
 
 
 
Step 6
 
Press “Share Status” to repost.
 
 
 
 
 
2. Notify your Family and Close Friends
 
Tell your Family and Friends about your Affiliate membership and let them know that you have a connection for Easy Approvals and Good Pricing for anyone they know that's looking for a vehicle. The more eyes and ears you have out there the better.
 
 
 
 
 
3. Mention Cash Back Options of up to $10,000
 
We have cash back options available on every deal. This can help them overcome financial obstacles such as unpaid vehicle registry fines, money needed for car insurance, money to pay off bills or high interest credit cards, vacation money etc.
 
 
 
 
 
4. Mention Trade-in Option
 
Be on the lookout for people who drive a 'crappy' vehicle and are currently in a high interest loan and let them know that you have a connection that might be able to upgrade their vehicle and keep their payments similar to what they are paying now. We accept all trades.
 
 
 
 
 
5. Be Persistent, Offer Incentives
 
They say that "Repetition is the mother of all skills" . It's easy for people to forget things with all the information they have to carry around in their heads on a daily basis. You should, therefore, give monthly reminders to the people you have already told.
Also, Offering them a little incentive might go a long way; such as "I'll throw you a hundred bucks if they get a car" or "I'll buy you a six pack of Kokanee", etc.
